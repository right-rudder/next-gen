## Understand the Test Format

- Multiple-choice questions
- Topics include aerodynamics, regulations, weather, navigation, and decision-making
- Minimum passing score: 70%

## Study Resources That Work

- FAA Airman Knowledge Testing Supplement
- Ground school courses (in-person or online)
- Redbird simulator for procedure practice
- Practice tests and question banks

## Build a Study Schedule

- Daily review sessions
- Focus on weak areas
- Combine book study with practical flight lessons

## Why Train at NextGen

- Access to modern simulators and study tools

## Final Tips

- Rest before the test
- Review key regulations and charts
- Trust your preparation
