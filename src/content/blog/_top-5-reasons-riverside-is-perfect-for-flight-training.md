Riverside Municipal Airport (KRAL) is more than just a training hub—it’s one of the best places in Southern California to become a pilot. Its ideal flying conditions and strategic location give students an advantage from day one.

## 1. Excellent Flying Weather

Riverside averages over 300 clear days per year. Consistent good weather means fewer cancellations, faster training, and more time in the air.

## 2. Ideal Airspace for Learning

Students train near major Southern California airspace, gaining experience communicating with ATC and navigating various air traffic environments.

## 3. Proximity to Key Landmarks

Flights often pass over scenic areas like Lake Mathews and Mount Rubidoux, making training both educational and inspiring.

## 4. Accessible Airport with Great Facilities

KRAL offers a full-service training environment without the delays of congested major airports.

## 5. Experienced Flight Instructors

NextGen Flight Academy provides structured programs led by FAA-certified instructors who guide students through each step.

## Ready to Start?

Riverside’s combination of weather, location, and training resources makes it a standout location for flight students.
