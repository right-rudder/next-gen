Southern California is known for its incredible flying conditions, making it a top destination for aspiring pilots. At NextGen Flight Academy in Riverside (KRAL) and Redlands (KREI), students benefit from ideal weather, diverse airspace, and year-round training opportunities.

## 300+ VFR Days a Year

With over 300 clear days annually, Southern California offers consistent opportunities to fly. Students can progress faster and maintain momentum without long delays due to poor weather.

## Real-World Airspace Experience

Flying near Class B, C, and D airspace helps students gain confidence operating in a variety of environments. Riverside and Redlands are ideally located to provide exposure to both controlled and less congested airspace.

## Scenic & Strategic Locations

Southern California flights often include views of the San Bernardino Mountains, Lake Mathews, and even coastal routes. This combination of scenery and proximity to major airspace builds practical skills and memorable experiences.

## Year-Round Training Advantage

Unlike regions affected by harsh winters or unpredictable weather, Southern California allows students to fly continuously. This consistency shortens training timelines and reduces overall cost.
